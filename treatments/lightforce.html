LightForce 3D-Printed Braces

The world's first fully personalized, 3D-printed bracket system — now available at inLine Orthodontics.

LightForce Product Image Placeholder

What Makes LightForce Different

Traditional braces use prefabricated, one-size-fits-most brackets. LightForce takes a completely different approach: every single bracket is custom-designed and 3D-printed from ceramic material to fit the unique shape of each individual tooth. This means more precise tooth movement from day one, with treatment that's designed around your anatomy rather than working around the limitations of generic hardware.

Dr. Darley digitally designs your treatment plan before a single bracket is placed, allowing him to visualize the end result and engineer the most efficient path to your ideal smile.

Faster Treatment

Treatment with LightForce averages 4–6 months shorter than traditional braces. Because the brackets are pre-programmed with the desired movements, teeth take a more direct path to their final positions.

Fewer Office Visits

The precision of custom brackets means less time spent on adjustments and fine-tuning. You'll spend less time in the chair and more time enjoying your life.

Discreet Appearance

LightForce brackets are made from tooth-colored ceramic material that blends naturally with your teeth. They're the most aesthetic bracket option available — discreet enough for adults and teens who want to smile confidently during treatment.

Precision Fit

Each bracket is custom-made to match the contours of your individual teeth, providing superior bond strength and more accurate tooth control compared to standard brackets.

How LightForce Treatment Works

Step 1: Digital Scan. Dr. Darley uses our iTero digital scanner to capture a detailed 3D model of your teeth — no messy impressions needed.

Step 2: Digital Treatment Design. Using LightForce's advanced software, Dr. Darley designs your complete treatment plan digitally, positioning every tooth in its ideal final location before treatment even begins.

Step 3: Custom Fabrication. Your brackets are 3D-printed from high-quality ceramic material, along with custom LightTrays (bonding trays) that ensure every bracket is placed in exactly the right position.

Step 4: Precise Placement. The custom LightTrays guide bracket placement for pinpoint accuracy — no guesswork, no manual positioning.

Step 5: Efficient Treatment. Because the system is pre-programmed with your tooth movements, treatment progresses efficiently with fewer adjustments needed along the way.

Is LightForce Right for You?

LightForce braces are suitable for a wide range of orthodontic cases, from mild to complex, and are an excellent option for both teens and adults. The best way to find out if LightForce is right for your smile is to schedule a free consultation with Dr. Darley.
