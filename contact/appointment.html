What to Expect

Your initial consultation is completely free and takes about 30–45 minutes. During your visit, Dr. Darley will:

  • Examine your teeth and jaw
  • Take any necessary X-rays
  • Discuss your treatment options
  • Provide a personalized treatment plan
  • Review financial options and insurance
