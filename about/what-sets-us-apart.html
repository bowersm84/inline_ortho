What Sets Us Apart

At inLine Orthodontics, every patient receives personalized care delivered with advanced technology, genuine compassion, and a commitment to excellence.

It's Never Too Late to Smile!

Our office works with patients of all ages. Our youngest patients start as early as five, and our oldest are well into their 80s!

We provide interceptive orthodontics in children to prevent problems from occurring. With our teenage patients, we make sure to achieve a healthy and beautiful smile once all their permanent teeth are in. Later in life, we can be a part of the team of specialists that works together to restore a mature dentition that needs more complex care.

A Unique Orthodontic Experience

You'll never experience cookie-cutter orthodontic care at inLine Orthodontics. We believe that everyone is unique and deserves to be treated that way!

Advanced Technology

We offer LightForce 3D-printed braces and Angel Aligners — cutting-edge treatments that deliver faster, more precise results with fewer office visits.

Coordinated Care

Dr. Darley has great relationships with dentists in the area and makes sure that your orthodontic care is well coordinated with your general dental care.

Bilingual Staff

Our office has bilingual staff members who speak Spanish, so our patients can communicate comfortably and we can explain all aspects of treatment clearly. Se habla Español.

Flexible Options

For busy patients, we offer treatment options that allow for less frequent visits and fewer emergency appointments. We use TADs (temporary anchorage devices) to treat complex tooth movement.

Affordable Care

We accept most insurances and offer in-house, zero-interest financial options, as we strive to make orthodontics possible for everyone.

Sports-Friendly

We offer free mouthguards to our patients who participate in sports, because protecting your smile is just as important as creating it.

No-Pressure Consultations

During your initial consultation at our Ocala orthodontic office, we will take our time to explain what we see in your X-rays, listen to your concerns, and then explain your treatment options in a way that is easy to understand. We want you to have all the information required to make an educated decision as to what treatment you want and what will give you the best result.

Our Dedication to the Ocala Community

We are proud to be part of the Ocala community and love supporting the people and organizations that have helped us get to where we are today. inLine Orthodontics is a proud sponsor of the Marion County Public School Foundation.
